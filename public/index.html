The next visualization is a study of the different types of battle strategies used in Game of thrones.
The three types of strategies are the Pitched Battle strategy, the siege strategy and the ambush battle strategy.

Thanks to this analysis of the battle strategies, we deduce that it is the Pitched battle which is the most used whereas this is less successful strategy with only 71.4% of success.
It is ultimately the ambush strategy that is most effective since it has a perfect percentage of success in this TV show followed closely by the Siege strategy.

The next visualization is an analysis of the entenne time of the main characters throughout the 7 seasons.
This study was intended to rank the main characters in this series thanks to the total number of minutes on the air but also to see their evolution season after season following the highlights of the story.

Three characters seem to stand out:
Jon Snow, Tyrion Lannister and Daenerys Targaryen.

The next visualization is an analysis of the entenne time of the main characters who died during the 7 seasons.
The objective of this visualization is to try to deduce a correlation between the accumulated airtime of the characters and the moment when they died in the Tv Show.

Thanks to this visualization we see that it is oftenly during the season where they are the most on screen that they are killed as we can see with:
Ned Stark, Stannis Baratheon, Oberyn Martell, High Sparrow, Twin Lannister and Robb Start
